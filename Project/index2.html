Welcome to My Homepage

Hello! I am a 5th year PhD student from Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University, co-supervised by Prof. Shengjin Wang and Prof. Qi Tian. I received my B.E from School of Life Sciences, Tsinghua University in 2010.

I am intereseted in computer vision and multimedia applications. Specifically, my recent works focus on large scale image retrieval and recognition tasks.

Highlights

We have provided the code for constructing the baseline used in our CVPR'14 papers.
