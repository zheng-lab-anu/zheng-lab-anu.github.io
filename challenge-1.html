One of the advantages of synthetic data is that data labelling is quickly, accurately and cheaply. however, the domain gap between real-world and sythetic data will influence the using of sythetica data. Therefore, how to design machine vision models that can effectively learn from synthetic data is worth exploring.

We are organizing the Alice-PersonX Challenge to help address these challenges by encouraging research and development into techniques that rely more on transfer learning and unsupervised approaches that do not learn from real-world data. It will focus on person re-identification problem.

Notice

The datasets for the Alice-PersonX Challenge, i.e., PersonX, Market-1501 and Market-T500 can be accessed now. The evaluation server is open again for submission of test results. Please register the chanllenge and follow the access instructions to get the data.
